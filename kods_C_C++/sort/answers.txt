sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?:  Performs the best in ideal case which refers to sorted array.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: For unsorted values merge will always perform the quickest.

How do you know?: Selective performs best when dealing with small arrays. When working with sorted data, even in
